Fostering leadership, inspiration, and growth among young individuals.

About YES

Youth Empowerment Summit (YES), is a flagship project of YELF dedicated to fostering leadership, inspiration, and growth among young individuals. YES serves as a dynamic platform where youth from diverse backgrounds come together to explore their potential, gain valuable skills, and ignite positive change in their communities.

Through a series of interactive workshops, engaging panel discussions, and inspiring keynote speeches, YES empowers participants to unleash their inner potential and take charge of their futures. From leadership development and entrepreneurship to personal growth and social activism, YES offers a rich array of sessions designed to equip youth with the knowledge, tools, and confidence needed to succeed in today's rapidly evolving world.

More than just a conference, YES is a catalyst for transformation and empowerment, connecting young people with mentors, resources, and opportunities to thrive. By fostering collaboration, creativity, and resilience, YES empowers participants to become the change-makers and leaders of tomorrow, driving positive impact in their communities and beyond.
