Youth Entrepreneurship & Development Training (YEDT)

Empowering youth with skills, knowledge, and resources to become the leaders and innovators of tomorrow.

About the YEDT Program

Youth Entrepreneurship and Development Training (YEDT) is a comprehensive program designed to equip young individuals with the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to thrive as entrepreneurs and leaders in today’s dynamic world.

Through YEDT, participants embark on a transformative journey of discovery, learning from seasoned experts and industry leaders about the fundamentals of entrepreneurship, business management, and innovation. From idea generation to market research, financial planning to marketing strategies, our curriculum covers all aspects of starting and growing a successful business.

More than just a training program—YEDT is a catalyst for personal and professional growth. Participants not only gain valuable knowledge and insights but also have the opportunity to network with like-minded peers, mentors, and potential investors. They receive ongoing support and guidance as they navigate the challenges and opportunities of entrepreneurship, ensuring that they have the tools and confidence to turn their dreams into reality.

At its core, YEDT embodies our organization's commitment to empowering young people to unlock their full potential and become agents of positive change in their communities. By investing in youth entrepreneurship and development, we are not only creating economic opportunities but also fostering innovation, resilience, and leadership among the next generation.

Key Highlights

Entrepreneurial Mindset

Learn the essentials of business innovation, idea generation, and creative problem-solving.

Networking & Mentorship

Connect with experienced entrepreneurs, industry experts, and potential investors for guidance.

Business Growth

Gain hands-on experience in financial planning, market research, and business development strategies.
