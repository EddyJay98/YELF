About YELF

Empowering youth for leadership, innovation, and community impact in Ghana and Africa.

Our History

YELF is a foundation established and registered in December 2012 with a vision of empowering the youth in Ghana and Africa. YELF is a dynamic youth empowerment organization dedicated to nurturing the next generation of leaders, innovators, and change-makers. At YELF, we believe that every young person deserves the opportunity to thrive, succeed, and make a positive impact on their communities.

YELF is driven by a shared vision of creating a world where every young person is empowered to create a brighter future for themselves and others. We believe in the power of collaboration, inclusivity, and innovation to drive positive change and transform lives.

Our programs and initiatives are designed to address the diverse needs and aspirations of young people, including academic support, career guidance, entrepreneurship training, mental health awareness, and social activism. Through workshops, seminars, mentorship programs, and community events, we provide a nurturing and supportive environment where youth can learn, grow, and thrive.

Join us on this journey of empowerment and transformation. Together, we can make a difference and shape a better world for generations to come."
