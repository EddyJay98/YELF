About- YELF

YELF is a foundation established and registered in December 2012 with a vision of empowering the youth in Ghana and Africa.

YELF is a dynamic youth empowerment organization dedicated to nurturing the next generation of leaders, innovators, and change-makers.

At YELF, we believe that every young person deserves the opportunity to thrive, succeed, and make a positive impact on their communities.

YELF is driven by a shared vision of creating a world where every young person is empowered to create a brighter future. We believe in the power of collaboration, inclusivity, and innovation to drive positive change and transform lives.
