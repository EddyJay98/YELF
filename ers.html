End of Year Examination Retreat (ERS)

Helping final-year SHS students master effective answering techniques for WASSCE.

About ERS

After the WASSCE, many SHS students are often highly convinced that the exam was well within their abilities and that they would pass with distinction. However, the results of the exam often fail to reflect such confidence. This has led us to believe that the answers provided by students often fall short of meeting the full requirements of the questions, thus preventing them from scoring the maximum marks allocated.

It appears that a majority of students either under-answer or deviate from the questions because they do not fully understand or grasp what is expected of them when answering examination questions.

YELF, having researched the causes of unexpected examination failures and seeking to contribute to improving the situation and supporting final year students sitting for the WASSCE, instituted the ERS in partnership with the Ghana Education Service. This initiative provides students with a free-to-attend but highly educational and insightful platform aimed at helping them learn the correct and effective methods of answering questions.
