Career Guidance & Counselling (CGC)

Empowering youth to discover their strengths, pursue their dreams, and achieve their full potential in the workforce.

About the Program

We understand the importance of guiding young individuals towards fulfilling and successful careers. That’s why we’ve instituted our Career Guidance and Counselling project — designed to equip youth with the tools, resources, and support they need to navigate the complex world of career choices.

Through personalized counselling sessions, young people explore their interests, skills, and aspirations. Our experienced counsellors provide insights and encouragement to help youth make informed career decisions.

Workshops & Events

We organize workshops, seminars, and networking events exposing youth to diverse career options and opportunities. From guest speakers to hands-on activities, our programs inspire and motivate youth to pursue their passions.

Resources & Mentorship

We provide access to online tools like career assessments, resume-building workshops, and job search assistance. Our mentorship initiatives ensure that every participant receives practical guidance to thrive professionally.
