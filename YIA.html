Program Goals

Five pillars that guide everything YIA does

Empowerment

Build practical farming & agribusiness capacity for youth.

Innovation

Promote digital tools & smart farming for higher yields.

Sustainability

Encourage eco-friendly practices that protect resources.

Entrepreneurship

Support youth agribusiness through mentoring & incubation.

Community Development

Foster collaboration between youth, farmers & local systems.

Program Components

Practical building blocks â€” training, incubation, and real-world learning

Training & Workshops

Hands-on training in crop & livestock production, agritech, financial literacy and farm business planning.

Agri-Innovation Bootcamps

Rapid idea-to-prototype bootcamps to develop tech-enabled agricultural solutions and climate-smart practices.

Apprenticeships & Mentorship

Field placements with experienced farmers and agripreneurs for on-the-job learning and mentorship.

Business support, pitch practice, investor introductions and small grants for promising youth enterprises.

Youth-led model farms that showcase best practices and act as local training hubs.

Exhibitions & Competitions

Annual showcases where youth pitch ideas, win grants, and connect with stakeholders.
